Quick reminders

Check-in will be on the lower level of The Ballantyne Hotel & Lodge before the Draft Party reception. If you are unable to attend the Draft Party, you may register Wednesday, May 13th, in the Quail Grill at Quail Hollow Club.

Your Wednesday Pro-Am credential will be issued to you at the Draft Party Registration. Please wear your credential at all times while on tournament grounds. The credential will be valid for tournament entry the entire week and provide you access to the Clubhouse and Quail Hollow Club grounds.

The play format will be "One Best Ball Net" and "Par is Your Partner" will be enforced by the tournament. If you can no longer make a net par on your ball, you will be required to pick up and proceed with your team as they finish the hole. This is done to speed up play, as length of time to play Pro-Am events is a major concern with the PGA Tour and the PGA Tour professionals.
